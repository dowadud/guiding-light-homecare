About Us

Serving Anderson with Heart and Dedication

Since our founding, Guiding Light Home Care LLC has been a trusted pillar of the Anderson, SC community. We understand that choosing home care is a deeply personal decision, and we're honored to be considered for this important role in your family's life.

Our team of compassionate, highly-trained caregivers is dedicated to providing exceptional care that promotes independence, dignity, and quality of life. We believe that everyone deserves to age gracefully in the comfort of their own home, surrounded by familiar surroundings and cherished memories.
